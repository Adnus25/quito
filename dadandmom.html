Dad and Mom

Dad and Mom met in college, where they were both studying . They were introduced to each other by a mutual friend, and they hit it off right away. Dad was struck by Mom's intelligence and beauty, while Mom was drawn to Dad's sense of humor and charm.

They started dating soon after and quickly became inseparable.they got busy but they always made time for each other.

After a few years of dating, Dad proposed to Mom on a beautiful beach in Hawaii, and she said yes. They got married a year later and have been happily married ever since.

Their love story is an inspiration to me and reminds me that true love really does exist. I am grateful every day to have them as my parents.
